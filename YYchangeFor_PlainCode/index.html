The Startup Scene

This project visualized reported investments and funding data between 1993 and 2013, according to the crunchbase dataset. Visualized by round and by currency, we see a proliferation of angel and seed investment after a bleak period following the recession. A large number of start-ups have kickstarted a culture of disruptive innovation.

How much money has been invested over time?

Investments visibly slowed down after the 2008 recession. Soon after, a new trend errupted, as seen from a high volume of low-value seed fundings. Investors started backing and betting on several new, innovative and disruptive technologies that they would hope would return a high ROI on an average-sized investment.
